Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy defines how RAOH Researchers handles data, transparency, and user trust. The platform is designed to function with minimal data exposure and maximum user autonomy.

1. Core Privacy Principle

RAOH Researchers operates on a strict privacy-first model. The platform is designed to function without requiring personal data from users. Your activity remains your own, with no profiling, identification, or monitoring.

2. Data Collection Policy

We do not collect, store, or process personal information from users browsing the platform.

This includes, but is not limited to:

• Names or contact information
• Email addresses
• Device identifiers or IP tracking
• Location data
• Browsing behavior or usage analytics

The platform is structured to remain functional without requiring user identification.

3. No Tracking Policy

RAOH Researchers does not use cookies, analytics tools, tracking pixels, or behavioral monitoring systems. No attempt is made to observe, record, or analyze how users interact with the platform.

Access is open and unrestricted, without surveillance or user profiling of any kind.

4. External Resources

The platform may include links to external academic and educational resources such as research databases, university portals, and curated learning materials.

Once you leave the platform, external websites operate under their own privacy policies and terms. RAOH Researchers does not control or assume responsibility for third-party data practices.

5. Telegram Community

RAOH Researchers may provide access to a Telegram community for academic discussion and collaboration.

Any activity within Telegram is governed entirely by Telegram’s own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We do not access, store, or manage user data from Telegram interactions.

6. Scope of Use

RAOH Researchers is intended for academic and educational use by students and researchers. The platform is not designed for commercial data collection or user profiling in any form.
